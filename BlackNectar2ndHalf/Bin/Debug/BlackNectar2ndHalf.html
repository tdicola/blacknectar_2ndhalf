BLACK NECTAR: SECOND HALF

by Tony DiCola

It wasn’t supposed to end this way. You were the best and the brightest—top of your class in college, captain of the rugby team, the youngest ever admitted into the space program. This was your first mission, your chance to make a small step in mankind’s conquest of the stars.

Then the accident happened. In the blink of an eye you’re cast away from the ship forever. The suit will pick up energy to prolong your trip, but you know there is no happy ending. They will write about you as a hero, a necessary casualty in the race to the stars.

There is no sadness or regret now. When your life functions cease your body will continue traveling forward. In time you will become the farthest trace of mankind in the galaxy. You will travel the vast expanses of space and return to the stars.

BLACK NECTAR: SECOND HALF

A sci-fi videogame in the style of home videogame consoles from 1976-1984. Inspired by music from The Insane Warrior's album We Are The Doorways—itself inspired by horror and sci-fi film scores of 1976-1984.

PROBLEMS?

Make sure you have the latest version of Microsoft Silverlight installed.
